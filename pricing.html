Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my data if I cancel?

It stays in your browser forever. CarrierEdge never stores your data on any server. Cancellation only removes access to member-only features — your P&L records, fleet data, and invoices remain exactly where you left them.

Do I need an internet connection?

Only for the initial load and optional live data (FMCSA lookups, EIA diesel prices, AI Advisor). Everything else — your P&L, fleet tracker, factoring tools — works fully offline once the app is loaded.

Can I try before paying?

Yes. Use the Free Factoring Tools with no account required. The full dashboard also loads with demo data so you can explore every screen before subscribing.
